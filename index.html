Genetic disorders

The intricate instructions within our DNA sometimes take unexpected turns. These changes, called mutations, can lead to genetic diseases. Inherited or spontaneous, they come in various forms, each affecting different parts of the body and causing a range of symptoms, from mild to severe. While there's often no cure, treatments can manage symptoms, and research continues to develop new solutions. If you have concerns, talk to a doctor or genetic counselor for guidance.
